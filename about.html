Souptik Das

Hi, I'm Souptik.

Just seven days before my IELTS exam, I was overwhelmed, struggling to focus, and unsure where to begin. With such little time left, I knew I couldn't afford to study everything. Instead, I took a step back and analyzed the exam itself: how questions were structured, what skills were being tested, and where marks could be gained most efficiently.

I broke the test down into question types and mapped them against my strengths and weaknesses. First, I focused on mastering the areas where I already performed well, ensuring I could secure those marks consistently. Then, I systematically worked on weaker areas, starting with the easiest improvements and progressing upward.

The week was intense and stressful, but I trusted the process, stayed calm, and executed the plan. The result was an overall IELTS Band 7.5.
